Grammar — Story Connectors

First · Then · After that · Finally · Also · However · Suddenly

7 min▼

🔗 Time Connectors — Tell a Story in Order

SEQUENCE — sıralama
First → ilk önce
Then → sonra
After that → ondan sonra
Finally → son olarak
EXTRAS — ek bilgi
Also → ayrıca
However → ancak / ama
Suddenly → birden / aniden
In the end → sonunda
📖 Example story:
Last Saturday, I woke up early. First, I had breakfast with my family. Then, we went to the market. After that, we met some friends for lunch. However, it started to rain, so we went home early. Finally, we watched a film together.
🇹🇷Hikaye bağlayıcıları: First (ilk) → Then (sonra) → After that (ondan sonra) → Finally (son olarak). However = ama/ne var ki. Suddenly = birden. Also = ayrıca.
💡
Good storytelling rule: Use at least 4 connectors in a story. They make your writing flow naturally — like a movie!
