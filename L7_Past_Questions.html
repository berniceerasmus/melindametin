Grammar — 3 Types of Past Questions

Did...? · Was/Were...? · WH-questions (What/Where/When/How)

8 min▼

❓ Type 1 — Yes/No Questions with DID

Did + subject + BASE verb → for all action verbs in the past

Did you go to the cinema? · Did she eat lunch? · Did they enjoy the film?
✗ Did you went? · Did she ate? — WRONG! Always BASE verb after did!
🇹🇷Did ile soru: Did + özne + fiilin yalın hali. "Did you go?" (gittın mi?) — "go" yalın hali, "went" değil!

❓ Type 2 — Yes/No Questions with WAS / WERE

Was/Were + subject + adjective/noun → for the verb "be"

Was the food good? · Was she happy? · Were they at the park?
✗ Did the food be good? — WRONG! Use Was/Were for "be"!
🇹🇷Was/Were ile soru: Was/Were önce gelir. "Was the food good?" = Yemek iyi miydi?

❓ Type 3 — WH-Questions (What / Where / When / Who / How)

WH-word + did + subject + BASE verb?
What did you eat? · Where did they go? · When did she arrive? · How did he feel?
WH-word + was/were + subject?
Where was she? · How was the weather? · What were they doing?
🇹🇷WH soruları: Soru kelimesi (what/where/when/how) + did + özne + yalın fiil. "Where did you go?" = Nereye gittin?

Short Answers ✅

Did you go? → Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.
Was it good? → Yes, it was. / No, it wasn't.
Were they there? → Yes, they were. / No, they weren't.

Common Mistakes ❌

✗ Did you went? → ✅ Did you go?
✗ Did she was happy? → ✅ Was she happy?
✗ Where you went? → ✅ Where did you go?
✗ What did she ate? → ✅ What did she eat?
