Homework 14: will 📝

Practise will and won't for predictions, promises, and offers.

⏱ ~60 min📝 100 points✅ 7 Sections

Match each sentence to its use. Write: prediction / promise / offer / decision

"I'll carry that for you!" →
"I think it will be cold tomorrow." →
"I will never lie to you." →
"I'll have the soup, please." →
"Will you help me with this?" "Of course I will!" →
"I don't think he will pass." →

📖 A Letter to the Future

Dear Future Me, I think life will be very different in ten years. I believe technology will be much more advanced. Cars probably won't need drivers, and robots will help in hospitals. I think I will live in a big city and I won't need a car because transport will be much faster and cheaper. I hope I will have a job I love — I think I will work in design or art. I won't forget the important things: my family, my health, and my friends. Whatever happens, I will try to be happy. See you in the future! — Zeynep

1. Zeynep believes cars will drive themselves in the future.

2. Zeynep thinks she will work in medicine.

3. Zeynep says she will buy a car in the future.

4. Zeynep mentions that she currently lives in a city.

5. Zeynep says family and friends are important to her.

✍️ My Future

Write 8–10 sentences about your future using will and won't. Include at least:

  • 2 predictions (I think / I don't think…)
  • 2 promises (I will / I won't always…)
  • 1 offer (I will help… / I'll…)
  • 1 spontaneous decision
