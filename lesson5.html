Grammar — Was vs Were: The Rules

Which subject takes was? Which takes were? Never confuse them again!

8 min▼

✅ Affirmative: Was / Were

WAS → I · he · she · it (singular)
I was happy. · She was tired. · He was late. · It was cold.
WERE → you · we · they (plural)
You were right. · We were excited. · They were at the park.
🇹🇷"be" fiilinin geçmiş hali: I/he/she/it → was · you/we/they → were

❌ Negative: Wasn't / Weren't

wasn't = was not → I · he · she · it
It wasn't cold. · She wasn't at home. · He wasn't ready.
weren't = were not → you · we · they
We weren't tired. · They weren't in the office. · You weren't late.
🇹🇷Olumsuz: was not → wasn't · were not → weren't (kısa form daha yaygın)

❓ Questions: Was / Were + subject

Was it sunny? · Was she happy? · Was the food good?
Were you tired? · Were they late? · Were there many people?
✗ "It was sunny?" — WRONG word order for questions!
🇹🇷Soru: was/were fiilini öznenin önüne taşı. "Was she...?" / "Were they...?"
💡
Memory trick: I was · he/she/it was → all singular = WAS. You/we/they were → all plural = WERE. Think: WAS = 1 person, WERE = 2+ people!
