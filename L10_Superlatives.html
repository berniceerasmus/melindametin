Superlatives 🏆

Learn how to say something is the best, biggest, or most exciting in a group.

🔥 Biggest & Best (3 minutes)

Can you answer these questions?

  • What is the tallest building in the world?
  • What is the longest river in Turkey?
  • Who is the most famous sportsperson in Turkey?

Try to answer in a full sentence: "I think the tallest building is..."

📌 How to Form Superlatives

Adjective typeRuleExample
Short (1 syllable)the + adj + -esttall → the tallest
Short ending in -ethe + adj + -stnice → the nicest
Short CVC endingdouble consonant + -estbig → the biggest
2+ syllablesthe most + adjectiveexciting → the most exciting
Adjective ending -ythe + change y → iesthappy → the happiest
Irregularspecial formgood → the best, bad → the worst
Structure: Subject + verb + the + superlative adjective (+ in/of + group)

✅ Volleyball is the most exciting sport in my school.
✅ She is the tallest player on the team.
✅ That was the best match of the year.
💡 Key difference: Comparatives compare two things. Superlatives compare one thing to all others in a group. Always use the before a superlative!
