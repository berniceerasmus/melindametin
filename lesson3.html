Grammar — What Are Irregular Verbs?

Why these verbs are special — and how to master them

5 min ▼

🧙 Regular vs Irregular

Regular verbs follow the -ed rule. Irregular verbs have completely different past forms that you must learn individually.

Regular: watch→watched · play→played · cook→cooked
Irregular: go→went · see→saw · eat→ate
🇹🇷Düzensiz fiiller — kurala uymayan fiiller. Her birini ayrı ayrı öğrenmek gerekir. Ama sadece 20 tanesini bilmek büyük fark yaratır!
💡
Good news! You already use one perfectly: eat → ate! The best strategy: learn each verb in a real sentence about YOUR life — that's how 'ate' became automatic!

Multiple Choice — Watch Out for Tricks!

📋 Choose the correct past form. Careful — some options look almost right! (Dikkat — bazı seçenekler neredeyse doğru görünüyor!)
1. "She ______ to the market in the morning." (go)
2. "I ______ a great series on TV last night." (see)
3. "He ______ a taxi to the airport." (take)
4. "She ______ goodbye and walked away." (say)
5. "I ______ the answer was wrong." (think)
